Webcrawlie is a web extension that visualizes all of the creepy crawlies (ads, trackers, and webcrawlers) that follow you across the internet.

For every ad, tracker, or webcrawler that your ad blocker - like uBlock, for example - detects, a little bug appears on your screen and doesn't go away.

This page is a mockup of that functionality.
